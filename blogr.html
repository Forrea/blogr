Introducing an extendable editor


Blogr features an intutive interface which lets you focus
on one thing creating content.The editor supports management
of multiple blogs and allow easy manipulations of embeds such as images,
videos and Markdown.Extensibility with themes and plugins provide
easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of a blog 





Robust content management


Flexible content management enables users to easily move through
posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding vustomized
categories,sections,format or flow.With this functionality,you are in 
full control

Free, Open, Simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large

commenting tools and works seamlessly with Google Analytics.The
architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.
